Spaghetti

Description

This is an amazing recipe for homemade sphaghetti! This is guaranteed to make your mouth water and fill your stomach. This recipe is great for parties, family dinners, and special occasions.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb sphaghetti noodles
  • 1 lb lean ground beef
  • sphaghetti sauce of your choice

Steps

  1. Bring 4 cups of water in a large pot to a boil.
  2. Take the sphaghetti noodles out, do not break them, and boil for ten minutes or longer. Stir occasionally.
  3. When noodles are halfway through boiling, pour sauce into a small pot, simmer on low heat.
  4. Pour noodles into strainer and rinse with cold water.
  5. Pour noodles back into pot, combine sauce, and enjoy!
