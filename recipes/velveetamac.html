Veleveeta Mac and Cheese

Description

A recipe for the classic velveeta mac and cheese! Very easy, very simple, and perfect for kids or even a quick lunch.

Ingredients

  • Velveeta cheese
  • Shell pasta

Steps

  1. Start boiling pasta. Boil for ten minutes, occasionally stirring.
  2. When pasta is tender, drain in sink.
  3. After draining, mix in Velveeta cheese and enjoy!
